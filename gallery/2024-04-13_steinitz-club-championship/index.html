2024 Club Championship

  • 2024 Club Championship

The 2024 Annual Steinitz Chess Club Championship took place in Cape Town on Saturday, April 13th, 2024. The event featured six rounds played at a rapid time control of 20 minutes plus a 5-second increment per move. Bringing together a strong field of club members, the championship provided an exciting day of competitive chess, highlighting both seasoned players and rising stars within the Steinitz community.
